# 🎬 Netflix Movies & TV Shows Dashboard

## 📌 Project Overview

The **Netflix Movies & TV Shows Dashboard** is an interactive Power BI dashboard designed to analyze Netflix's content library. It provides insights into titles, genres, IMDb ratings, release trends, countries, and content types, helping users explore the Netflix catalog through visual analytics.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

* Analyze Netflix Movies and TV Shows.
* Identify the most common genres.
* Understand IMDb ratings and votes.
* Explore content release trends over the years.
* Compare Movies and TV Shows.
* Visualize content distribution across countries.

---

## 📂 Dataset

The dataset contains information about Netflix titles, including:

* Title
* Type (Movie / TV Show)
* Genre
* Country
* Release Year
* IMDb Score
* IMDb Votes

## 📊 Dashboard Features

### KPI Cards

* 📺 Total Titles
* ⭐ Average IMDb Score
* 👍 Average IMDb Votes
* 📅 Start Year
* 📅 Present Year

### Visualizations

* Top Genres
* IMDb Score Distribution
* Total Shows Released by Year
* Movies vs TV Shows (Pie Chart)
* Total Titles by Country
* Interactive Search Filter

---

## 📈 Key Insights

* Netflix contains **5,805** titles in the analyzed dataset.
* The average IMDb score is **6.53**, indicating generally positive audience ratings.
* Most content has been released after the year **2000**, with rapid growth in recent years.
* Movies make up a larger share of the catalog compared to TV Shows.
* Drama and Comedy are among the most common genres.
* Content is distributed across multiple countries, highlighting Netflix's global reach.
